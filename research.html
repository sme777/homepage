Labor Migration and Economic Growth

The effects of labor emigration and immigration on the economic development of the state.

Emigration of workers is often considered a negative influence on the local economy of the workers, especially if the workers leaving the state are highly skilled. Similarly, the immigration of skilled laborers is taken as a positive externality. I am particularly interested in the behavioral aspect of the choice to migrate. What is the underlying reasoning that workers choose to leave, and what is the condition that would push them to come back. I have currently surveyed both of these condition for Armenia, which has more diaspora than local folks. A sample questionnaire can be found in the link below.

Computer Security in ML

Facial recognition in video surveliance and corrupted GPS signals.

Computer Security in ML is as important as the ML algorithms, if not more. My focus as of now is on video surveillance and signal processing applications. Facial recognition has been one of the pivotal points in ML development. It has also given rise to a more subtle issue; tricking the system. Another common application of ML is GPS tracking. With the inevitable delays in signal communications, ML plays a crucial role in providing a predicted location at any given time. With enough knowledge on how this GPS location is predicted, malware could manipulate this prediction. An example would be the transportation of important cargo or airplane control (Malaysian Airlines Flight 370).

Networks and Signals

The combination of the underlying structure of the network with signal processing.

Graphs and Networks are a prime part of today's world. Whether it's the (co)movement of stock prices or a network of your friends, the ideal representation is often a graph. Combining signals with a structure of graphs yields a rather inconspicuous result, the processing of which is often referred to as graph signal processing. My interest include defining shift on graphs and identifying properties of these structures that would put them in the linear time invariant systems.
