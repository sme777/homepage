Labor Migration and Economic Growth

The effects of labor emigration and immigration on the economic development of the state.

Emigration of workers is often considered a negative influence on the local economy of the workers, especially if the workers leaving the state are highly skilled. Similarly, immigration of skilled laborers is taken as a postive externality. I am particulalry interested on the behavioral aspect of the choice to migrate. What are the underlying reasoning that workers choose to leave, and what are the condition that would push them to come back. I am currently conducted a survey on both of these condition for Armenia, which has more diaspora than local folks. A sample questionaire can be found in the link below.

RISC-V Architecture

The greatest thing that happened to computer architecture in the 20th century.

The introduction of RISC-V ISA set the foundations of the open source computer architecture. The transperency allows optimization in any of the desired field, i.e. power consumption, secruity etc. I find RISC thrilling and full of potential for a widespread use. Currently, I am designing a microprocessors that would best suit for the drone project. At the start, Logism was used to come up with the implementation of the intrusction set, and soon after I transitioned to using Verilog. Preliminary research shows that the microprocessors that works best with the drone is one with optimized power consumption and performance.

Networks and Signals

The combination of the underlying sturcture of the network with the signal processing.

Graphs and Networks are a prime part of today's world. Whether it's the (co)movement of stock prices, or a network of your friends, the ideal representation is often a graph. Combining signals with a structure of provided by a graph yields a rather inconspicous structure, the processing of which is often reffered to as graph signal processing. My interest include defining shift on graphs and identifying properties of these structures that would put them in the linear time invariant systems.
