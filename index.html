Research Interests


NP = P

The solution is trivial, when N = 1.

My life-long dream is to prove NP = P. I carry an independent research trying to optimize infamous NP complete problems. Currently I am working on the Traveling Salesman Problem. The idea behind reducing time complexity of the Traveling Salesman Problem is partitioning given graph and using graph spectral theorem.

Entropy Power

Better Entropy Power Inequality for multiple vectors in Rn vector space.

We have a model for well-behaved random variables first proved by Claude Shannon. The entropy inequality N(X+Y) ≥ N(X) + N(Y) holds for two well-behaved random variables; what happens if we lift this restriction, or introduce more random variables. This research attempts to find a more general entrop power inequality for N random variables.
